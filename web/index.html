About

Made by operators, for operators.

When a section gets cut from a track, the timecode show has to collapse with it — delete the cues inside the removed window and slide everything after it left, by the exact length of the cut. MA2 Timecode Cut does that ripple across every subtrack at once and hands you a file grandMA2 re-imports without a fight.

Byte-exact

BOM, namespace, tabs, line endings — everything but the cut is preserved, so the import never chokes.

Musical, not just seconds

Cut by whole bars at the track BPM, or frame-accurate by timecode. A live preview shows exactly what moves.

Stays on your machine

Everything runs in the browser. Nothing is uploaded — no server ever sees your show.
